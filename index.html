About me

I studied Romance Languages in college because I loved the way they sounded, the culture they held on to, the stories they told. As a student in literary translation, I discovered just how much I also loved finding the similarities between languages and, of course, their inherent and frustratingly undeniable differences. I loved solving the seemingly impossible task of recreating an author's words in another language.

Programming has combined my love of languages with the creative and artistic freedom not just to re-create, but to create. I love learning the different languages, but more than that, I love the entire process of actually making things. I thoroughly enjoy finding the problems (that I have often created myself) and then solving them--everything from having to throw 7 debuggers into the code to addressing all the compilation errors only to find errors in logic. I love the feeling of having created a fully-functioning program, and I love that the work never feels finished.
